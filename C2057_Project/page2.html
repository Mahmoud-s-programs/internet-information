Web Technologies and Standards

Technology Description Usage
Internet of Things (IoT) The Internet of Things (IoT) refers to the network of physical objects embedded with sensors, software, and other technologies for the purpose of connecting and exchanging data with other devices and systems over the internet. IoT technology is employed in smart home devices, such as thermostats and security cameras, allowing users to control their home environments remotely and monitor for safety and efficiency.
Artificial Intelligence (AI) Artificial Intelligence (AI) encompasses computer systems designed to mimic human intelligence, learning from data to make decisions, recognize patterns, and perform tasks. AI is utilized in various applications, including virtual assistants like Siri and Alexa, predictive text and autocorrect features in smartphones, and in the healthcare sector for diagnosing diseases and personalizing treatment plans.
Blockchain Technology Blockchain technology is a decentralized digital ledger that records transactions across multiple computers in a way that ensures security, transparency, and immutability. It is widely used for cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin, enabling secure and transparent financial transactions without the need for traditional banking intermediaries.
